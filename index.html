Haldia Lions Model School

This school was established by Haldia Lions Club in the year of 1995 and is located in Purba Srikrishnapur, under Purba Medinipur district, West Bengal. The school aims to provide proper education to children from Pre-Primary stage onwards on the basis of strong principles and values in our country’s tradition. The school provides necessary instruments to make learning interesting thereby developing a child physically, mentally and emotionally to become an integrated well informed personality needed for great social change. The school provides pre primary education to children from the age group of two plus and so on and the success of the school depends on the cooperation and support of the parents.
